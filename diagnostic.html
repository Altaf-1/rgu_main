A state-of-the-art diagnostic laboratory runs under the aegis of the Royal School of Medical and Allied Sciences. The diagnostic laboratory caters to the requirements of the students pursuing a diploma and B.Sc. programme in Medical and Laboratory Technology (MLT). In addition, the laboratory also extends support services to the community at large through the Royal Swasthya Seva, which offers free medical services. The reports generated by the lab are used to check the primary health status of the individuals.

Objective

  • Give practical experience to our students of MLT, Nursing, Pharmacy, and other related departments.
  • Check the health status of the staff and students of RGU at a minimal cost.
